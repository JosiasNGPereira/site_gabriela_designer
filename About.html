About

Gabriela Pereira

I am an Interior Designer driven by a passion for crafting spaces that seamlessly blend beauty with ergonomic efficiency. With a keen eye for aesthetics and functionality. Whether embarking on a residential renovation or starting a design from scratch, I am dedicated to creating environments that not only inspire but also enhance the way people live and work. Throughout my career, I have refined my skills in organisation, teamwork, and creative design solutions to deliver exceptional results. From conceptualisation to execution, I work closely with clients to understand their visions to life while ensuring comfort and practicality in every design.

Given the evolving nature of my career, I continually seek opportunities to incorporate my preferred styles - influenced by Japanese and Scandinavian aesthetics - into my design approach. Drawing inspiration from the minimalism of Japanese design and the functionality of Scandinavian style, I bring a unique perspective to each project, infusing cultural influences into every space I create. My approach is simple: to design not just spaces, but lifestyles that inspire and endure, seamlessly blending elements of Japanese and Scandinavian styles into Contemporary and Modernist Designs.

With a solid foundation in project management principles, I am adept at guiding clients through each phase of the design process, ensuring smooth execution and surpassing expectations. My experience enables me to navigate complexities effectively, delivering successful outcomes while continuously refining my skills. In summary, my goal is to design spaces with intentionality, crafting environments that uplift and inspire. I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate with you on your next project and transform your vision into reality
